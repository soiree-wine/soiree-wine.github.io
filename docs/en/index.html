Concept

Discover the unique flavors of Japanese wines. Experience the perfect harmony of Japanese wines and cuisine.

Soirée is a bistro where Japanese ingredients meet wines from around the world. Our sommelier, a blind wine-tasting champion, carefully selects over 4,000 bottles to create the perfect pairing for your meal. Enjoy a unique dining experience that will take you away from your everyday life.

Located in the tranquil Sendagaya district of Shibuya, within easy reach of Kita-sando Station on the Fukutoshin Line, we offer a unique opportunity to delve into the fascinating world of Japanese wine.

Producer

Connecting Wineries with You

The quality of Japanese wines is rapidly improving, and we want more people to know about their charms. This is the reason we started this store. I myself have visited wineries all over Japan, and I want to convey what I felt there to our customers.

We also hold events such as winery owner dinners to take advantage of our network and make the wineries our goal. Enjoy Japanese wines at soirée as a new experience.

Store ManagerAyumu Shida
